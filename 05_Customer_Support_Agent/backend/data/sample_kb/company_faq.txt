Company: TechCorp Support Knowledge Base

REFUND POLICY:
Customers can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase. After 30 days, refunds are not available but store credit may be issued. To request a refund, the customer must provide their order number and reason for the return.

SHIPPING & DELIVERY:
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days. International shipping takes 10-15 business days. Orders over $100 qualify for free standard shipping.

ORDER TRACKING:
Customers can track their order by visiting our website and entering their order number in the tracking section. Tracking information is available 24 hours after the order is dispatched.

ACCOUNT ISSUES:
If a customer cannot log in, they should use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If the issue persists, our team can manually reset the account. Accounts are locked after 5 failed login attempts for security reasons.

PRODUCT WARRANTY:
All products come with a 12-month manufacturer warranty. The warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not cover physical damage, water damage, or unauthorized modifications.

CANCELLATION POLICY:
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After 2 hours, the order enters processing and cannot be cancelled. If you need to cancel after this window, you will need to wait for delivery and then initiate a return.

CONTACT SUPPORT:
Support is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. Email: support@techcorp.com. Phone: 1-800-TECHCORP. Average response time for emails is 24 hours.
